# Privacy Policy for Pong

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview
Pong is a pong game tracking and rating app for Dartmouth students. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Google Account Information
- Email address (@dartmouth.edu only)
- Name
- Google profile information

### Game Data
- Game results and scores
- Player ratings (ELO)
- Match history
- Friend connections

### Profile Information
- Year of graduation
- Dartmouth affiliation

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Authenticate your identity
- Track and display game statistics
- Calculate player ratings
- Enable social features (friends, leaderboards)
- Improve the app experience

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers and is only accessible to you and other authenticated Dartmouth students using the app.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data outside of the Pong app
- Use your data for advertising

We DO share:
- Your name, year, and affiliation with other app users
- Your game results and ratings within the app
- Leaderboard rankings with other users

## Third-Party Services

We use:
- **Google Sign-In** for authentication
- **Railway** for hosting our backend
- **Expo** for app distribution

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Request deletion of your account and data
- View your stored data
- Opt out of friend requests

## Children's Privacy

This app is intended for college students only. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
