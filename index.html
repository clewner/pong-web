Pong Support

About

Pong is a pong match tracking app for Dartmouth students. Track your games, compete with friends, and climb the leaderboard.

How to Use

Sign In: Use your Dartmouth Google account (@dartmouth.edu)

Create a Game: Select 4 players (2v2 format) and create a match

Submit Results: All players vote on the winner

Track Progress: View your ELO rating and match history
